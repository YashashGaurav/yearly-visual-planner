yearly-visual-planner

A year-view planner for Google Calendar

Open Planner →

Shows events from all calendars selected in Google Calendar. Toggle individual calendars on or off using the buttons at the bottom of the planner.

Privacy Policy

When you open the planner, you'll be asked for two permissions:

  1. View your calendars — displays events in year-view. No data is transferred, stored, or shared.
  2. Manage its own config in Google Drive — saves settings across sessions. No access to your Drive files.
